Wrive

Simple Google Drive docs publishing

  1. Update your document's sharing permission to Public or Anyone with a link.
  2. Get the document's file id and append that to Wrive's url.

    Example:

    https://docs.google.com/document/d/1OZ9...uMYA/edit {{ url_for('index', file_id='1OZ9...uMYA', _external=True) }}
